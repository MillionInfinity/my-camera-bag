Welcome to My Camera Bag

My Camera Bag is a tool for photographers to save lists of and reference their photography gear. Create a profile, add your gear, and begin organizing your gear into 'camera bags' so you have all the info you need to help you prepare for your photography shoots. Create a different bag for each different type of shoot, scenario, enviorment you work with.
